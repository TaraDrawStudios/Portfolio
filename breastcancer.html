Breast Cancer AI Detection

Research Team

The Breast Cancer AI Detection project is a research project that focuses on using artificial intelligence to detect breast cancer in medical images. This project involves training a few machine learning models on a dataset of mammogram images to identify patterns and features that are indicative of breast cancer. The goal of this project is to develop a tool that can assist radiologists in the early detection of breast cancer, ultimately improving patient outcomes and saving lives.
