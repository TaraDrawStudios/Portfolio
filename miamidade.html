Miami Dade Sign In

A Perfect Upgrade

This project is a simple sign-in sheet application that I created for my mother who is a known teacher at Miami Dade public schools. The application addresses the concern of keeping track of sign ins. For teachers, in order to get paid, they must sign in to the miami dade protal however, with all the stress of papers and students, sign ins can be easily forgotten and there is no way to go and check if you signed in or not. This application allows teachers to keep track of their sign ins by providing a simple interface where they can log their sign ins and view their history in order to get paid.
